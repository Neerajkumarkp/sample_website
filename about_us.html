At K K DIAGNOSTIC CENTRE, we are dedicated to providing accurate, reliable, and comprehensive diagnostic services to support better healthcare outcomes. With state-of-the-art technology, a highly qualified team, and a patient-first approach, we strive to set new standards in pathology and laboratory testing.

SATISH JADHAV

FOUNDER

As the visionary behind K K DIAGNOSTIC CENTRE, Satish Jadhav has always been passionate about delivering accurate and reliable diagnostic solutions that empower both doctors and patients. With 15 years of hands-on experience in the field of pathology and diagnostics, he has developed a deep expertise in medical imaging and laboratory testing. Holding a qualification in XYZ and specializing in X-ray diagnostics, he founded K K DIAGNOSTIC CENTRE with the mission to provide timely, precise, and high-quality test results. His dedication to early disease detection, preventive healthcare, and advanced diagnostic techniques has made the center a trusted name in the industry, ensuring better treatment outcomes for all

SHAMIKA JADHAV

CO-FOUNDER

Shamika Jadhav, the Co-Founder of K K DIAGNOSTIC CENTRE plays a crucial role in managing laboratory operations and maintaining strict quality control standards. Her expertise ensures that every test is conducted with the highest level of accuracy, efficiency, and ethical standards, making K K DIAGOSTIC CENTRE a trusted center for precise and reliable diagnostic services. Her dedication to quality assurance and patient care helps maintain excellence in laboratory operations.
